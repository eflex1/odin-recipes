Bread

Description

Bread is a staple food made from flour, water, and yeast. It is baked into soft, fluffy loaves and enjoyed worldwide in different forms.

Whether served with butter, used in sandwiches, or eaten on its own, bread is a versatile and delicious part of many meals.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Knead again and place in a loaf pan.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes.
