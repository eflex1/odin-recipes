Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular and versatile dish enjoyed worldwide. It typically consists of cooked rice stir-fried with vegetables, eggs, and your choice of protein, all seasoned with soy sauce and other spices.

This dish is perfect for using leftover rice and can be customized with various ingredients to suit your taste. It's quick to prepare and makes a delicious meal any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced onion and minced garlic, saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Push vegetables to the side and pour in the beaten eggs; scramble until cooked through.
  5. Add the cooked rice and stir everything together.
  6. Pour soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice mixture; mix well to evenly coat.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
