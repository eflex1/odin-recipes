Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and spices. It's often served at parties and family gatherings.

Rich in flavor and color, jollof rice pairs well with fried plantains, meat, or eggs, making it a crowd favorite across countries like Nigeria and Ghana.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 tomatoes (blended)
  • 1 red bell pepper (blended)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cubes seasoning (Maggi/Knorr)
  • Salt and chili to taste
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, pepper, and half the onion together.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry the chopped onions.
  3. Add tomato paste and blended mixture. Cook until it thickens.
  4. Add seasonings, salt, and chili.
  5. Add washed rice and water. Cook on medium heat until done.
  6. Stir occasionally to avoid burning.
